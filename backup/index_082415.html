The Beginning

Koffee’s initial startup was successfully funded in November of 2014 through the popular crowd funding site; Kickstarter. 100 indivuals collectively put together $30,000 in 30 days, proving that there is a strong desire for a healthier coffee. Koffee concentrates its rich flavor in a 6 ounce espresso style sipper. Koffee Reinvented is the first of a series of portable health beverage drinks Kafé Reinvented will bring to the market. We plan to innovate within each health beverage market, both creating and revolutionizing the portable healthy bottled beverage industry.

The Founder

Koffee was inspired by Kody Chaet, an educational plant-based chef and lifestyle transformation coach, who sought to grow the world through the art of healthier food and beverage. He grew up a vegetarian and avid animal activist near Yellowstone, Montana. As a young entrepreneur, Kody pursued multiple careers in the health and wellness industry. He started n Portland, OR as a personal fitness trainer and smoothly transitioned into the private chef world in Los Angeles, CA. After a decade as a health and fitness professional, he decided he wanted to spread his gift of healing to the world as a whole. Kody realized the best way to spread his message of health to the masses, was to reinvent everyones favorite health beverages, starting with coffee, the most popular beverage on the Planet Earth!

  • Cold Brewing is an ancient Peruvian process Todd Simpson brought it to America in 1964 from Peru. It is done by steeping coffee grounds for a extended a period of time vs. short bouts. In a nutshell, time replaces heat. Because hot water doesn't’t penetrate the coffee gournds, the acidic oils from the coffee bean are not released and transferred into the liquid mixture. This creates anywhere from 65% - 80% less acidity. Heat brewed coffee is usually at a PH of 5 or lower. For every one ounce of coffee drink you would need to drink 20 ounces of water to completely neutralize acidity created in the body. Koffee strives to keep the pH between 6 & 8, which means our coffee ranges from minimally acidic, neutral or “pH balanced”, to slightly alkaline. Because acidity is connected to many degenerative illnesses, This means our low acid Koffee is easier on the stomach, joints and energy levels.

  • The Rainforest Alliance Certified seal assures consumers that the product they are purchasing has been grown and harvested using environmentally and socially responsible practices.   • Traditionally, all coffee was shade grown. As the coffee beans mature more slowly in the shade, natural sugars increase and enhance the flavor of the coffee. Shade grown coffee is most often organically grown, free of chemical use. Shade grown coffee requires little or no chemical fertilizers, pesticides or herbicides. The shade trees also filter carbon dioxide which causes global warming, and aid in soil moisture retention which minimizes erosion. Shade grown coffee is not only better for the environment, but also for earths creatures. Migrating bird populations have been in rapid decline since the introduction of "sun" coffee and the consequent destruction of rainforest for more coffee plantations. As many as 150 species of birds have been identified on shade coffee farms. Shade coffee farms are, for the most part, organic and sustainable.
